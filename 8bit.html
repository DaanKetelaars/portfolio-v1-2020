Case info

For our class New Product Development we had to come up with a product for students during the COVID-19 situation. We had 3 design challenges we could choose from, me and my team (Sebas van de Munt, Jewel Buenventura and myself) chose the design challenge gezelligheid (cosiness, social). We went through a whole process of ideation to get to our end product. In the end we had to create 2 posters showing our process and concept.

Role(s)

Daan Ketelaars: Poster design, desk & field research.
Sebas van de Munt: Illustrations, poster design, desk & field research
Jewel Buenventura: Poster design, desk & field research, productbio

After several ideation techniques and research we came up with the 8-bit stream lounge. You can enter this stream lounge with your own character and then watch films, YouTube videos or series with your classmates/friends. The 8-bit style gives it that fun and old school game effect most people love. In this lounge you can really make it your own and edit several things. Just a moment to sit back, relax and still be able to hang out with your classmates, like you did before the whole COVID-19 situation came up.
