Who am I, what drives me and what are my hobbies?

Hi, my name is Daan Ketelaars Coreen, a 22 yo digital designer & front-end developer. At the moment I am in my 2nd year of Communication & Multimedia Design at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. I've also done a 4 year MBO education as interactive designer at Mediacollege Amsterdam. I wasn't done with learning (I never will haha) and studying, so I went on to my bachelor degree and might even go on to get my masters as well, while I'm at it.


What drives and interests me?

Well, when starting a new project or working on a project, focusing on the attention to detail and making sure everything is worked out to perfection. In addition, the fact that I can really bring ideas to life, pixel by pixel is something that motivates me and keeps me striving for more.

I really developed a huge interest for graphic design, art direction, typography, colours, copy and composition/layout. When you have a good and clear understanding of these skills you can come a long way in building great digital/ non digital products. I would love to learn more about these skills and try to master them.
