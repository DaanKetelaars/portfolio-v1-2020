Case info

Project Sport was our first team project of the year one of CMD. My team (Mehmet Ayvaz, Mila Dors, Jort Broekhof, Carmen Roozendaal and myself) were given a sport to create an application for. Luckily for me, we got the sport I have been doing for over 16 years, field hockey. We got a little help in defining our concept. Our concept had to be something that could help pro field hockey athletes stay in good shape and injury free, despite their busy sport and work/study schedule.

Role(s)

Daan Ketelaars: Visual design, prototype, desk & field research
Mehmet Ayvaz: Visual design, prototype, desk & field research
Mila Dors: Desk & field research, productbio
Jort Broekhof: Desk & field research, prototype
Carmen Roozendaal: Desk & field research, productbio

After our first week Slessure was born, but after a few iterations and low/me-fi prototypes, something was missing. So after loads of extra research, interviews, expert interviews and iterations we came to the conclusion to just focus on the sleep pattern and keeping track of their training. At the moment the app was mainly focused on injuries and sleep, but it still had some flaws. That's when we decided to change it up a bit and move to sleep/training. The athletes can now share their results with the physio, coach and trainer. The staff could see these results in their own version of the application as well.
